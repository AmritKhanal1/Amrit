Program Overview

BE Civil Engineering

The first semester of Civil Engineering at Purbanchal University establishes core scientific principles. Students cover 19 credit hours across subjects including Applied Mechanics, Chemistry, and Engineering Drawing, providing the technical foundation required for structural design and material science in future semesters.
