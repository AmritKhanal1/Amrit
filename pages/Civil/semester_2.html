Semester Overview

Civil Engineering - 2nd Semester

The second semester introduces core civil engineering concepts like Building Technology and Applied Mechanics (Dynamics). Students also dive deeper into engineering mathematics and physics, while learning professional drafting through Building Drawing and AutoCAD, which are vital for practical site work and design.
