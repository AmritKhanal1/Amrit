Semester Overview

Civil Engineering - 3rd Semester

The third semester marks the beginning of core professional engineering subjects. Strength of Materials and Fluid Mechanics form the backbone of structural and hydraulic design. Surveying introduces essential fieldwork skills, while Engineering Geology provides the understanding of the earth's surface necessary for construction in Nepal's unique terrain.
