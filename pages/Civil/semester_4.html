Semester Overview

Civil Engineering - 4th Semester

The fourth semester advances into the analytical and practical design phases of civil engineering. Theory of Structures-I and Hydraulics introduce complex analysis techniques for structural stability and fluid flow. Survey-II completes the essential training in precision measurement, while Concrete Technology provides the foundational knowledge of the most widely used construction material in Nepal.
