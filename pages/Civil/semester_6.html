Semester Overview

Civil Engineering - 6th Semester

The sixth semester shifts the focus toward comprehensive design and infrastructure management. Design of RCC Structures and Foundation Engineering provide the expertise needed for constructing safe and durable buildings. Irrigation and Sanitary Engineering address essential community needs, while Engineering Economics introduces the financial decision-making required to lead successful engineering projects.
