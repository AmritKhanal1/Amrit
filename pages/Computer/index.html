What is Computer Engineering (BCE)?

Computer Engineering (BCE) is a four-year undergraduate program that uniquely **combines computer science and electrical engineering** principles. The comprehensive curriculum covers core areas such as programming, software development, computer networks, database management, web technologies, and system design.

This section of ShikshaFlow is dedicated to providing comprehensive, semester-wise resources** for the entire 8-semester BCE program. You will find lecture notes, practical lab manuals, final project reports, and essential reference materials from various leading universities in Nepal.
