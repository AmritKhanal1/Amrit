B.E. & B.Arch Entrance Overview

The Engineering Entrance Examination is the gateway to prestigious institutions in Nepal. For B.E. (Bachelor of Engineering), the focus is strictly on PCM and English. For B.Arch (Bachelor of Architecture), candidates must additionally clear an Aptitude test involving drawing and creative visualization.
